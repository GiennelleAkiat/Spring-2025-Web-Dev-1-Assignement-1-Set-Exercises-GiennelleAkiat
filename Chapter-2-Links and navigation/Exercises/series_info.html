Lies of P

About

Lies of P is a thrilling soulslike game that takes the story of Pinocchio, turns it on its head, and sets it against the darkly elegant backdrop of the Belle Epoque era.

Plot

You are a puppet created by Geppetto who‚Äôs caught in a web of lies with unimaginable monsters and untrustworthy figures standing between you and the events that have befallen the world of Lies of P.

You are awakened by a mysterious voice that guides you through the plagued city of Krat - a once lively place that has been poisoned by madness and bloodlust. In our soulslike, you must adapt yourself and your weapons to face untold horrors, untangle the unfathomable secrets of the city's elites and choose whether to confront predicaments with the truth or weave lies to overcome them on the journey to find yourself.

A lot of people say this game is just completely ripping off the souls games but I think it has enough uniqueness to distinguish itself from other soulslikes. The undodgeable, unblockable red attacks are an interesting mechanic that is really rewarding when done successfully and really punishing when messed up. The vinyl records you can get are just amazing as well, just gorgeous music. The implementation of puppet parts that function as your armor is also a nice touch because this allows you freedom in what outfits you can wear making it so you don't have to sacrifice fashion for defense. I also saw another reviewer mention how the enemies have clearly choreographed attacks because they are puppets which is true to some degree, but you'll find that puppets aren't the only enemies in the game and on top of that a lot of puppets will sometimes short circuit in between some attacks messing up their easily readable attacks which was honestly a nice touch. If you enjoy soulslike games I would highly recommend this one, as it is probably the best soulslike I have ever played besides the soulsborne games.
